ROBOTIC/ARM

ABOUT THE ROBOTIC ARM

this project involves designing, building, and programming a robotic arm to perform various automated tasks with precision and control.


Objectives

• To understand the mechanics and electronics behind robotic arms

• To learn programming for controlling robotic movements

• To apply automation principles in practical scenarios

Sorting Three Different Colored Blocks

This functionality showcases the robotic arm's ability to recognize and sort blocks by color. It identifies red, green, and blue blocks and places them in corresponding color-coded areas

Stacking Three Blocks

This functionality demonstrates the robotic arm's precision control by picking up three blocks from designated locations and stacking them one on top of the other.
